Summary of the Ad Moderator Terms

 

BY SELECTING “Accept the T&Cs” YOU SIGNIFY THAT YOU HAVE READ, UNDERSTOOD, AND AGREE TO THE FOLLOWING:

 

When You register with Us as an Ad Moderator, You will be able to watch Ads submitted by Ad Builders to assess their compliance with the acceptable content Criteria shown in the Jifflr Ad Builder FAQs.

 

In the event an Ad does not meet the Criteria, You must reject the Ad by selecting the non-compliant Criteria, indicating that You have deemed the Ad unsuitable for Review through the App.

 

Each Ad shall be reviewed by at least two Ad Moderators.  If an Ad You have determined to be acceptable is found non-compliant by the second Ad Moderator (known as a “Conflict”), the Ad shall be submitted to a third Ad Moderator (a “Deciding Moderator”). The Deciding Moderator shall have the deciding vote on whether the Ad is compliant or not (i.e. whether the Ad becomes a Moderated Ad).

 

If You are subject to more than 3 Conflicts in a calendar month which result in the Deciding Moderator disagreeing with Your determination, We reserve the right to suspend or terminate Your status as an Ad Moderator.

 

As an Ad Moderator, You may earn Cash for Your Moderated Ads. The Rate for each Moderated Ad will vary from time to time without notice and at Our absolute discretion.  The amount of Cash You earn shall be calculated at the end of each calendar month. 

 

You will conduct Your duties as an Ad Moderator in an honest and truthful manner.

 

Please refer to the full Jifflr Terms for more information.
